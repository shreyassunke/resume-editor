# V2 — Fintech / Data Science

## Purpose

For roles at the intersection of money, data, and engineering. It leads with the
Stripe payments work on HUMN AI and surfaces financial infrastructure (KTP) and data
research (DSAIL) higher in the document than the SWE version does, so payments and
analytics signal appears before the AI projects.

## Targets

- **Roles:** Fintech SWE Intern, Data Science Intern, Quant/Analytics Intern
- **Companies:** Stripe, Remitly, Accolade, Gravity Payments

## ATS keywords this version leads with

payments, APIs, financial modeling, data pipelines, SQL, Python, analytics, Stripe,
fraud detection, risk, compliance, ETL

## Bullet-ordering rationale

- **Section order** is Education -> Experience -> Leadership & Research -> Projects ->
  Skills. KTP (financial infrastructure) and DSAIL (data research) move above ATLAS so
  finance/data credibility lands early.
- **HUMN AI** leads with the Stripe payments/PCI bullet, then the full-stack
  architecture bullet (with cost analysis, signaling analytical thinking), then REST
  APIs/marketplace, then architecture/CI-CD for credibility.
- **KTP** is expanded to two bullets to strengthen the finance narrative.
- **Skills** surface Python, SQL, and Pandas first, with Data Pipelines, Financial
  Modeling, ETL, and Payments & Billing in Concepts.
